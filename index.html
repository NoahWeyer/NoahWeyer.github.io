Problem Statement: Budget/Gas Mileage

When starting college or any new facet of life, it easy to overlook finances especially when it comes to investments such as cars. Our solution is to create a way for our users to track the monetary investments they put into maintaining their vehicle such as basic repairs, gas mileage, and oil changes.
